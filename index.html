The Microfiber Difference

Norwex cleans differently than other cleaners. There are three ways to clean:

  1. Steam, which is often inconvenient
  2. Chemically, which is dangerous and often toxic
  3. Mechanically, which is the Norwex difference

The microfiber cloths can pick up 99%* of bacteria using only water, and most Norwex products contain BacLock technology that allows the cloths to self-purify as they dry. The best part is that the mechanical clean is safe, nontoxic, and it really works! Check out the demonstration with raw chicken! Think of how much safer your house could be by removing bacteria, making your surfaces sparkle, and removing toxic cleaners! Think of how much time and energy you could save by allowing the microfiber to do the scrubbing for you! You could cut your cleaning time in half!
